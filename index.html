Who Are We?

We Sanparavahi (Sand painting)would like to introduce to you a Unique creation, which is created from the underground natural coloured stone powder, sea sand and river sand. These collections are 100% natural colourful stones and sand are taken from various places. Those stones are grinded into powder and this is the raw material of our pure handmade masterpiece creations like Black sand art and Multi colour sand art. This product is absolutely eco friendly and highly durable in colour(there is no chance of fading colour) because of no artificial colours or dyes are added.
